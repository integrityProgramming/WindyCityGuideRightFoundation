The Windy City Guide Right Foundation is a registered 501(c) 3 founded in 2021 with the express purpose of providing annual scholarships to students that attend Chicago State University in Chicago, Illinois. Additionally; through its mentorship program the foundation provides a conduit to established professionals worldwide who offer advice and guidance to selected students which improves their opportunities for internships and career opportunities in a multitude of career fields.

The Foundation was established by alumni brothers that attended the university and were initiated into the Theta Zeta Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Incorporated along with other chapters after graduation. The foundation is operated by a volunteer board.
